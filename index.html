Who is Little Krishna?

Little Krishna is the beloved child of Vrindavan, known for his mischievous smile, divine miracles, and boundless love. From stealing butter to lifting Govardhan Hill, his stories inspire joy and devotion across generations.

Divine Tales

  • Makhan Chor: The butter thief who stole hearts
  • Kaliya Mardan: Dancing on the serpent to protect Vrindavan
  • Govardhan Leela: Lifting a mountain to shelter his people
  • Raas Leela: The divine dance of love and unity
